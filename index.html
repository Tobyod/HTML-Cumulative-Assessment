What is Geometry Dash?

Geometry Dash, commonly refered to as gd, is a platforming rythme developed by the swedish developer Robert Topala. It is avaliable on pc, and android and iOS devices.

The Appeal of Geometry Dash

Geometry Dash is known for it's extensive level creation system, and the ridiculously high difficulty of it's levels. These features draw in incredibly creative players and those who desire a challenge.
