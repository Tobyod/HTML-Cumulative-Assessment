How to Rapidly Improve Your Geometry Dash Playing skill

Things to Consider When Starting Out

Players in Geometry Dash can generally split into one of three categories those being star grinders (players who focus on playing a bunch of levels approved by the game's developer), demon grinders (players who focus on beating levels with the hardest difficulty in the game, which is known as demon) and challenge players (players who focus on playing incredibly difficult short levels).

What is the fastest way to improve?

The best way to improve at playing the game is to be a challenge player by playing a bunch of challenges. Because Challenges have some of the hardest gameplay in any level due to their short length they are by far the best way for new players to rapidly improve. Another incredibly useful tool to improve is demon, and challenge progression lists. These lists clearly show which levels you should practice to improve in game skills, and give a clear route of progression for newer players.
